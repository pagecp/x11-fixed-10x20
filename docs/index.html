From monochrome X terminal to M4 Max

Christian Pagé adopted 10×20 around 1990 and never found a terminal font he could read as effortlessly. He converted it when Mac OS X appeared, exporting FixedRegular with FontLab 3 in 2003, and carried it through every generation of his working environment. The exact Apple bitmap SFNT has now been recovered from its resource fork and is distributed beside a broader Unicode companion.
